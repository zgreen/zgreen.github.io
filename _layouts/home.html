Current position

I'm currently employed as a front-end developer at MIT, working in the libraries.

I'm primarily responsible for the MIT Libraries' website, libraries.mit.edu.

About the job

libraries.mit.edu runs on Wordpress (using Multisite). Most of my days are spent doing development and maintenance on this site.

Version 2 of the site, which I developed, was just recently launched.

You can view the github repo for the site here.
